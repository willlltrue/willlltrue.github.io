Plane Hijack Game

Score: 0

Game Rules and Controls:

You are piloting a hijacked plane. Your goal is to avoid obstacles and survive as long as possible.

  • Use the Arrow Keys to control the plane's direction:
    • ↑ (Up Arrow): Move the plane upwards
    • ↓ (Down Arrow): Move the plane downwards
    • ← (Left Arrow): Move the plane to the left
    • → (Right Arrow): Move the plane to the right
  • Press Spacebar to start the game or shoot at the enemies.

Avoid colliding with red obstacles and survive as long as possible!
